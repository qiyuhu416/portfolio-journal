<h2 id="curiosity">§2 · That mindset of having fun </h2>

Before going deeper, I think it's important to ask: why are we learning AI.

No need to have a clear answer, but understanding acknowleding the real driving force is important. 

I've chased AI tools because of FOMO, and I knew it was because of FOMO. 


So many many many people have mentioned "you need to find your real passion for work." I know the importance, but passion is something you can't have if this is just not your passion. 
For a long time I thought i might just be too ideal, until I saw people who are genuinelly passionate about their work, and said "I woudl still want to do this job even if I was not getting paid" (Samar K). (link in quote).

The "Palo alto" video i created was purely for fun, even if it is not getting used anywhere, but it is just fun to create something out that i WANT to create.

I guess that was why i was willing to spend the whole day to explore a new tool just to create it, because using the tool now has a goal.

Now that vibe coding and many other tools has made creation much easier, i can create more, if i want.

To my future self & whoever reading this, I hope i can remember that feeling of having fun, especially when AI comes out and many people were creating.
