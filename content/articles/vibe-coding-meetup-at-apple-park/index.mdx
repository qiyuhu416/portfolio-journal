<h2 id="why">§ Why</h2>

In October 2025 I started noticing a pattern: more and more of us were using vibe-coding tools — Claude Code, Figma Make, the usual suspects. Almost always it was engineers doing it. And because of the privacy constraints inside the company, we couldn't just flip a switch and let every team try them.

So I organized an informal meetup. Lunch, Apple Park, no agenda. Just people who were curious, who wanted to chat about what they were trying, what was working, what they were scared of.

<Figure fig="01" placeholder alt="Apple Park meetup" caption="Lunch, a round of introductions, someone's laptop open in the middle of the table." />

The thing that surprised me: it was never about the tools. It was about finding the people who were thinking out loud about the tools. Those are different rooms.
