<h2 id="why">§ Why</h2>

I love connecting people. I want my friends to be friends with each other. I don't want work to be the laptop-and-the-meeting loop — eight hours of getting-things-done and then going home.

So I joined a lot of channels at Apple. Japanese language. Tennis. AI. Whatever small Slack room had people gathering around something that wasn't the job. Most of the people I'm glad I met, I met through those channels — not through the work I was hired to do.

<Figure fig="01" placeholder alt="Channels and coffee chats" caption="A collage of the channels, screenshots, and the coffees that came out of them." />

It turns out *"have fun at work"* is a design choice you make on yourself.
